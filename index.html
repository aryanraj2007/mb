Get To Know Aryan

Aryan Raj is a technology enthusiast in middle school who finds solitude in computers - his favourite activities include programming, playing video-games, and camping. He has a stellar academic record - recently being accepted into NJHS. His extracurricular activities include GEMUN, Tennis, Guitar, and Boy Scouts - in which he is a life scout and ASPL.

Aryan's achievements include being the runner up best actor in the one-act play program. He has also been awarded the CMSE Star Student Award in 6th and 7th grade and has been accepted into the NJHS for the 2020-2021 school year.

Aryan spends his spare time programming - he is fluent in HTML, CSS, and Boostrap - Gaming - he plays Minecraft and Fortnite - playing the guitar and participating in boy scouts.
